About Contourlines

Contourlines is a free, open-source interactive contour map of Earth and sea level rise simulator. It renders real NOAA elevation and ocean depth data as a classic topographic map — isolines, hypsometric tints, hillshading — that scrolls seamlessly around the planet and morphs into a 3D globe when you zoom out.

How to use it

  • Drag to pan — the map wraps endlessly, east to west.
  • Scroll or pinch to zoom. Fully zoomed out, the map becomes a globe.
  • Drag the sea level slider to raise or lower the ocean — the scale is exponential, spanning the full planet from the Mariana Trench (−11,000 m, oceans fully drained) to Mount Everest (+8,848 m, everything submerged). Coastlines, contour lines, and hypsometric colors all re-map to the new waterline instantly, while a crimson reference line keeps marking where today's coastline used to be.
  • Try the presets: −120 m shows Ice Age coastlines with land bridges like Beringia and Doggerland; +70 m shows the world if all ice melted.

Frequently asked questions

What would happen if all of Earth's ice melted?
Sea level would rise about 70 meters. Set the slider to +70 m and watch low-lying regions — the Netherlands, Bangladesh, the Nile delta, Shanghai, southern Florida — disappear beneath the new coastline.
What was sea level during the last Ice Age?
About 120 meters lower than today. At −120 m you can walk from Siberia to Alaska across Beringia, and Britain joins Europe across Doggerland.
How accurate is this?
Elevations come from NOAA's ETOPO1 Global Relief Model at 5-arc-minute resolution (~9 km per cell). The simulation is a static "bathtub" model for education — it ignores tides, storm surge, isostatic rebound, and coastal defenses.
Does it track me or need a server?
No. Everything runs locally in your browser with WebGL2. No cookies, no analytics, no accounts.

关于 Contourlines

Contourlines 是一个免费开源的交互式世界等高线地图和海平面升降模拟器。 它把 NOAA 真实的陆地高程与海底深度数据渲染成经典的地形图 —— 等高线、分层设色、山体阴影 —— 地图可以东西方向无缝循环拖动，缩到最小时会变成一个三维地球。

使用方法

  • 拖拽平移地图 —— 东西方向无限循环。
  • 滚轮或双指捏合缩放。缩到最小时，地图变形为地球。
  • 拖动海平面滑杆升高或降低海面 —— 滑杆采用指数刻度，覆盖整个星球： 从马里亚纳海沟（−11000 米，海洋完全排干）到珠穆朗玛峰（+8848 米，全部淹没），越往两端 拖动变化越快。海岸线、等高线和分层设色全部以新水面为零点即时重算；一条红色参考线始终 标注今日海岸线的原位置，新旧海岸线之间就是被淹没（或新生）的土地。
  • 试试预设：−120 m 重现冰河时期的海岸线（白令陆桥、多格兰）； +70 m 展示所有冰盖融化后的世界。

常见问题

如果地球上的冰全部融化会怎样？
海平面会上升约 70 米。把滑杆拉到 +70 m，就能看到荷兰、孟加拉、尼罗河三角洲、上海、 佛罗里达南部等低地被新海岸线淹没。
冰河时期的海平面是多少？
比今天低约 120 米。在 −120 m 时，西伯利亚与阿拉斯加之间的白令陆桥、英国与欧洲大陆之间的 多格兰都会浮出海面。
模拟有多精确？
高程数据来自 NOAA ETOPO1 全球地形模型，分辨率 5 弧分（约 9 公里/格）。这是一个用于科普的 静态"浴缸"模型，不考虑潮汐、风暴潮、地壳均衡回弹和海堤。
会跟踪我或需要服务器吗？
不会。一切都在浏览器本地通过 WebGL2 运行，没有 Cookie、没有统计、没有账号。
